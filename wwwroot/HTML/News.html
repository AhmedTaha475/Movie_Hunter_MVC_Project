Taron Egerton

Taron Egerton is set to lead the new AppleTV thriller, Tetris. It's the true story behind the invention, marketing, and battle over the blockbuster video game Tetris licensing rights. Egerton plays Henk Rogers, a Dutch video game developer who joins forces with the Russian inventor of the game, Aleksei Pajitnov to wage war with the Russian authorities to get the rights to produce the gaming phenomenon and sell it to the rest of the world.
